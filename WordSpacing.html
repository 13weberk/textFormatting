Word Spacing

Use CSS to replicate the pictured eye chart. To accomplish this:
  1. Adjust size, text-alignment and word-spacing on each of the spans (topLine, secondLine, thirdLine, etc) to adjust the display of letters in that span
  2. Adjust the padding and margins of the eye chart div to roughly match the dimensions of the eye-chart and the placement of text on the chart.
  3. Adjust the margins of paragraphs within the chart to fine-tune the appearance of rows of text. NOTE: You will probably need to add ids to some of the paragraphs and adjust their margins individually to completely replicate the chart as pictured.
  4. If you replicate the appearance of the letters on the chart, and want to add the green and red lines, try adding a bottom border to the appropriate paragraphs and setting it to a very large size
  5. If you finish all this, try to find a google font that approximates the font shown in the chart, add it to this document, and apply it to the eyeChart div.
